10 February 2022

SinguloBot updated:

  • Now multiple medibots can heal you during the round!
  • Fixes modular consoles teleporting intellicards/IDs to cyborgs and AIs who try to eject them
  • Emergency pod helmets no longer provide flash protection.

05 February 2022

Ivniinvi, timothymtorres, Azarak, mickyan, updated:

  • Fixed pAI loudness booster to work when not in holoform
  • Fixed garbage collection for songs, making the game slightly faster
  • You may now change the sound of certain instruments with a selection of appropriate variations, similarly to the piano synth
  • Fixed accordion not working
  • Adds a hidden fun instrument

Phil Smith updated:

  • A small commemoration to our former LRP server, golden, is now on the captain's laser gun.

SinguloBot updated:

  • Revision information is once again burned into dd.log, SSFeedback's revision information has been hard disabled as a temporary fix.
  • humans always deal normal punch damage to mobs, instead of having weird damage calcs depending on the mob subtype
  • attacking borgs with a punch that deals over 10 damage damages them
  • bees now have 6 hp
  • pacifists can no longer attack aliens
  • fixes alien eggs from disappearing before hugger spawns
  • Explorers now require 3-days on the server and 5-hours of Crew experience before they can be selected
  • Banning panel suppression check is now silent
  • People no longer get stuck on walls of an empty z-level.

11 January 2022

Pirill, kit-katz updated:

  • Adds three new pAI holochassis: carp, bee and phantom!
  • Adds inhands and head-worn sprites for all other pAI chassis that were missing them

01 January 2022

SinguloBot updated:

  • The *spin and *flip emotes now cause a small amount of confusion.
  • Spin and flip confusion now properly checks for intent.
  • Unintentional spins and flips no longer cause confusion.
  • Cyborg mesons work similarly to human mesons by showing dark areas

27 December 2021

SinguloBot updated:

  • Arena controller no longer leaks admin auth tokens. As a result the follow button has been removed from the UI.
  • Security records console no longer contains an href exploit
